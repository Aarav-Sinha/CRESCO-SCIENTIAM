Mission Briefing

Cresco Scientiam means "I grow through knowledge."

Now in its fifth deployment, Cresco Scientiam is Delhi Public School Megacity's flagship interschool science festival — a two-day convergence of forensics, chemistry, mathematics, mechanics, design, strategy, and engineering operations, staged as twelve independent dynamic events.
