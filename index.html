Incoming Transmission

A fest built on three verbs, not one theme.

Cresco Scientiam began as a single-day science meet at DPS Megacity and has grown, edition after edition, into a two-day arena where science, storytelling, and strategy collide. Every event traces back to one of three instincts: the instinct to create something from nothing, the discipline to establish it under scrutiny, and the nerve to compete for it on the day.

  • 01A city-wide science fest hosted by Delhi Public School Megacity, Kolkata.
  • 02Twelve events across Mythology, Cosmos and Eco — from chess to genetic engineering.
  • 03Five editions strong, growing from four events in 2022 to twelve in 2026.

    Pick Your Arena

    Three worlds, one signal.

    Every event this year sits inside one of three theme-worlds. Each has its own rules of engagement — but all of them ask the same question: what have you built, and can you defend it?

    WORLD 01

    Mythology

    Strategy, storytelling and performance drawn from the epics — where an ancient board game sits next to a modern debate stage.

    • Kurukshetra Chess
    • Chitrakatha Pictionary
    • Dharmasangram Debate
    • Swaranjali Music
     WORLD 02

    Cosmos

    Pure STEM — chemistry as art, forensics as detective work, mathematics as sport, and a quiz that rewards speed of thought.

    • Chromatica Chem Art
    • Corpus Vestigium Forensics
    • π·Infinitum Mathlympics
    • Astra Cognitio Quiz
     WORLD 03

    Eco

    Science with a conscience — drama, journalism, genetics and architecture all built around one question: what do we owe the planet?

    • Clamor Naturae Drama
    • Viridis Vox News Reading
    • Genesis Nova Genetics
    • Ventus Lapidum Architecture
